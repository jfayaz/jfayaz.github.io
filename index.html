JAWAD FAYAZ

I am a researcher in the field of civil and earthquake engineering, working on seismic ground motion analysis, decision support systems for earthquake early warning, hazard analysis, infrastructural system analysis, and risk and reliability analysis. My research projects are mainly based on advanced statistical analysis, feature engineering, time-series analysis, machine learning and deep learning.

Besides research and teaching, I like practicing martial arts (Muay Thai and Jiu Jitsu), long-distance running, weight training, hiking, gaming, listening to informative podcasts (such as Joe Rogan Experience, The Knowledge Project etc.) and reading inspiring books (such as Man's Search for Meaning by Victor Frankl, Beyond Good and Evil by Friedrich Nietzsche, etc.)
